### Handyman ⚠️ Minor Issues
**Current:** General Repairs & Odd Jobs / Baby-Proofing / Safety Install / Furniture Assembly

**Issues:**
- "Baby-Proofing" and "Safety Install" are two separate line items but one booking on every platform — always listed together
- Missing: Hanging (art, mirrors, shelves) is the #1 booked handyman task on Thumbtack
- Missing: Caulk / Seal / Weatherstrip — very high volume, especially seasonal

**Recommended:**
- General Repairs & Odd Jobs
- Furniture Assembly
- Hanging, Shelving & Mirrors *(new)*
- Baby-Proofing & Safety Install *(merged)*
- Caulk, Seal & Weatherstrip *(new)*

### Interior Carpentry ⚠️ Minor Issues
**Current:** Trim & Baseboard Install / Built-Ins & Shelving / Stair Repair & Railing / Custom Woodwork & Framing

**Issues:**
- "Custom Woodwork & Framing" conflates two very different skill sets — rough framing (structural, licensed) vs. custom finish woodwork (millwork, decorative). On Angi these are separate categories entirely.
- Crown molding is a top searched item — fits under Trim but worth naming explicitly

**Recommended:**
- Trim, Baseboard & Crown Molding *(rename)*
- Built-Ins & Shelving
- Stair Repair & Railing
- Custom Millwork & Finish Carpentry *(rename — removes "framing")*

### Insulation ⚠️ Minor Issues
**Current:** Batt & Blown-In Insulation / Spray Foam / Weatherstripping / Air Sealing

**Issues:**
- "Weatherstripping / Air Sealing" is in the wrong group — on every platform this is under Handyman, not Insulation. Insulation contractors don't do weatherstripping.
- Batt & Blown-In doesn't specify location — professionals list by area (attic vs. wall/crawlspace) because pricing and access differ significantly

**Recommended:**
- Attic Insulation (Batt & Blown-In) *(rename/split)*
- Wall & Crawlspace Insulation *(new)*
- Spray Foam
- Air Sealing *(keep — remove weatherstripping, that goes in Handyman)*

**Recommended:**
- Brick, Stone & Veneer
- Stucco Application / Repair
